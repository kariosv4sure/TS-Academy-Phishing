TS Academy — Phishing Evidence Report

Responsible disclosure report · Prepared by Ifeanyichukwu Collins Chibueze
Executive Summary

This document contains evidence of a phishing campaign impersonating TS Academy. The campaign solicits an "application fee" via a third-party payment integration and uses URLs that do not belong to the official TechStudio Academy domain. This report includes captured emails, screenshots, URL/hosting information, and recommended mitigation steps.

Key Findings
  • Impersonation: Phishing emails/pages use the TS Academy brand with non-official URLs.
  • Unauthorized payment flow: Victims are directed to Paystack-hosted checkout via fake tracking links.
  • Urgency & social pressure: Emails use deadlines and limited-time language to force quick payments.
  • Brand risk: Risk of user loss and reputational damage to TS Academy.
Timeline
  1. User clicks social post and registers
  2. Received automated confirmation emails linking to fake URLs
  3. Received "scholarship award" email prompting an application fee
  4. Investigation: domain checks, logo mismatch, payment flow inspection
  5. Reported findings to official TechStudio Academy contact via WhatsApp
Immediate Risk

High — users may pay money into the scam, exposing financial and personal data.
